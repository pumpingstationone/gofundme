


##  1. Please provide background on your organization, including key team members, geographical area, and industry of focus.

### Background on Pumping Station: One

Pumping Station: One is a community-driven makerspace and tech incubator that fosters creativity, collaboration, and innovation. Our organization is centered around the idea that by providing access to tools, resources, and a diverse community of artists, makers, engineers, tech enthusiasts, and entrepreneurs, we can amplify the potential of individuals and help them turn ideas into reality.

### Geographical Location

We are located in the Avondale neighborhood of Chicago, which is known for its eclectic mix of industrial spaces, residential areas, and a growing community of creatives and tech professionals. The neighborhood is home to a diverse population, including young professionals, artists, tech enthusiasts, and families, many of whom are drawn to the neighborhood for its vibrant culture and proximity to downtown Chicago.

Avondale has become a hub for small businesses, innovative startups, and entrepreneurial ventures, making it an ideal location for Pumping Station: One. Our makerspace not only serves as a creative outlet for local residents but also contributes to the revitalization of the area, attracting individuals who seek to collaborate, innovate, and create in an environment that encourages growth and diversity.

### Key Team Members

Our team is composed of a dedicated board of directors, volunteers, and members from a wide range of professional backgrounds, including technology, engineering, design, manufacturing, and the arts. This diversity allows us to bring unique perspectives and skill sets to the table, making our makerspace a place where cross-disciplinary collaboration thrives. Key team members include:

- Board Members with experience in software engineering, hardware development, product design, and community building.
- Volunteers who dedicate their time and expertise to facilitate workshops, provide mentoring, and maintain equipment.
- Community Leaders who help guide strategic decisions and ensure that Pumping Station: One remains inclusive, accessible, and focused on fostering innovation.

### Industry Focus

Our primary industry focus lies in technology, manufacturing, and the creative arts. We offer cutting-edge tools and workspaces for entrepreneurs, small business owners, and independent creators working in fields such as:

- Advanced Manufacturing – CNC machines, 3D printers, laser cutters, and more to support prototyping and small-batch production.
- Electronics & IoT Development – Providing resources for electronics design, microcontroller programming, robotics, and hardware development.
- Digital Fabrication – Supporting artists, designers, and creators with tools like 3D printers, sewing machines, and digital design equipment.
- Sustainability – Partnering with organizations focused on recycling, upcycling, and clean tech, such as Precious Plastic, which transforms waste into valuable products.
- Addressing the Skills Gap – Providing training and hands-on experience in manufacturing, engineering, and digital fabrication, ensuring that individuals gain the technical skills needed to enter and thrive in high-growth industries.

### Diversity & Inclusion

One of the core values of Pumping Station: One is its commitment to inclusion and diversity. Our membership and board are composed of individuals from a wide range of socioeconomic backgrounds, racial and ethnic groups, and gender identities. We strive to create an environment where everyone feels welcome, supported, and empowered to innovate and contribute.

Our diverse membership base includes students, hobbyists, professionals, and entrepreneurs from all walks of life, from low-income individuals who may not otherwise have access to these resources, to industry experts who mentor others and help shape the direction of our space. This mix of backgrounds and experiences fosters a sense of community, promotes shared learning, and leads to exciting collaborations that reflect the rich diversity of our city and its people.

### Conclusion

Pumping Station: One is a community-driven hub for creativity, technology, and innovation, located in the Avondale neighborhood of Chicago. With a focus on fostering collaboration and providing access to tools and resources, we support a diverse community of individuals who come together to share knowledge, solve problems, and create impactful solutions. Our inclusive approach ensures that people from all backgrounds, cultures, and socioeconomic statuses have the opportunity to innovate and contribute to the future of technology and manufacturing.

- Skill Development: Feedback surveys will be used to measure the skills and knowledge gained by participants in training programs. Success will be determined by the percentage of participants who report increased technical skills and applied learning.
- Certification & Workforce Development: We will also track the number of individuals who gain certifications in specialized areas (e.g., CNC machining, welding, digital fabrication) or secure jobs in the tech or manufacturing industries post-training.

### 3. Community & Collaboration Metrics

- Collaborative Projects: Success will also be measured by the number of collaborative projects and partnerships formed within the space. We will track cross-disciplinary collaborations among entrepreneurs, engineers, designers, and community organizations, focusing on the creation of new products, startups, or solutions.
- Nonprofit & Educational Partnerships: Monitoring the growth in the number and scale of collaborations with local nonprofits, educational institutions, and industry partners will help measure the success of our community-driven approach.

### 4. Economic Impact

- Startup Creation & Job Growth: We will track the number of new startups formed within the space, as well as job creation within these startups. These metrics will reflect the broader economic impact of the capital investment on the local tech and manufacturing ecosystem.
- Product Launches & Commercialization: Success will also be measured by the number of products or innovative technologies developed in our space that are successfully launched or commercialized.
- Investment & Funding Secured: Tracking the amount of capital raised by startups in our space, either through venture capital, angel investments, or crowdfunding, will reflect the growing value of the resources we provide.

### 5. Member Satisfaction & Impact Surveys

- Member Feedback: Regular member satisfaction surveys will be used to gauge the perceived value of the upgrades, tools, and training programs. We will ask members to rate the usefulness of the space, tools, and resources, and their ability to accomplish their goals.
- Community Impact: Periodic surveys will assess the broader social impact of our activities, asking members about the space’s role in their personal development, professional growth, and ability to give back to the community.

### 6. Sustainability & Environmental Impact

- Eco-Friendly Practices: We will monitor the reduction in material waste and energy usage as a result of sustainable practices, such as recycling initiatives (e.g., Precious Plastic) and energy-efficient equipment. Success will be evaluated by the volume of material recycled and energy savings achieved.

### Conclusion

By regularly tracking these key performance measures—ranging from tool utilization and training participation to startup creation and environmental impact—we will be able to assess whether the capital investment is meeting its objectives. We will use this data to refine our programs, continuously improve our services, and ensure that we are maximizing the positive impact of this project on both the local tech ecosystem and our broader community.
